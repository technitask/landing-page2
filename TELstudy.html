Integrated Offer Management System

75% reduced time to market of new offers with integrated offer management and data analytics system

The Opportunity

The marketing department at a National Telecommunications Company was using two different systems to manage acquisition and retention offers. In 2013, with the push for Big Data, the organization realized they needed more flexible offer creation and management capabilities to effectively and easily target customers and maximize revenue. These capabilities were required for both new and existing customers.

The Solution

Our solution included the convergence of the two offer management systems into one. In addition to this, we proposed integrations to a real-time data analytics package that provided information about the customerâ€™s usage and bills over the last few months. This provided the firm with the ability to easily configure offers for customers in real-time and apply them immediately for quick market responses.

The Result

The new offer management system provided the company with the ability to target customers by various metrics. It gave the many channels a single source for all offers. The system also solved the problems relating to slowness and outages during popular handset launches. The solution resulted in a 75% decrease in the time to market of new offers, allowing the organization to quickly respond to competitor offers and reduced the impact of competitor promotions on sales.
