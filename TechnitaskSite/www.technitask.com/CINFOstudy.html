Customer Information Systems

Delivered integrated SAP CRM implementation ahead of schedule with seamless migration to the new system

The Opportunity

One of the major provincial utility providers required a replacement to their antiquated Customer Information System. This would mean seamlessly transferring an existing system to a newly designed and complete system. We led the client’s CIS replacement project (i.e. SAP CRM implementation) technical team.

The Solution

The solution was to generate the technical architecture, SAP BASIS technical and all IT infrastructure for the project and to monitor operations of the Project Management Office environments.

The Result

All technical components for the program were delivered ahead of schedule. The delivery included the procurement, deployment, and management of all IT infrastructure, building, configuration and management of all SAP environments, performance management, and disaster recovery planning.

Technitask

Technitask provides IT management, applications, services, processes and people to the utility industry.
