As an IT contractor our customers hire us to perform specific tasks. Our people are process experts in those tasks, bringing efficiency, repeatability, reliability, auditability and traceability to the business and technology process. By marrying the technical delivery with the business process we are able to deliver the best customer solutions. All our work is appropriately documented with process flows, business requirements, business solution docs, technical architecture and design, technical configuration, implementation and change logs. Processes and solutions are appropriately tested in depth prior to deployment and then a process improvement process monitors and continually improves the process driving ongoing efficiency.
