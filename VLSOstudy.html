Driver Licencing Services Overhaul

Delivered streamlined processes and created an integrated set of systems that deliver over 1.2M tests every year at 95 locations

The Opportunity

Legacy systems and manual processes were used as part of daily operations in transportation services until 2014. We identified several key areas where technology could help drive operational improvements including inefficient hiring and training cycles, lost time to manual processing, data entry errors resulting in penalties, and poor operational reporting and monitoring capabilities.

The Solution

The solution comprised of commercial off-the-shelf applications as well as custom built applications, along with an integration layer that seamlessly assembled the disparate systems to achieve business objectives.

The Result

We delivered an integrated suite of systems that resulted in an minimal training and ramp-up time for agents and seamlessly integrated into existing processes to make it easier for agents to follow. A customer flow management system was also introduced that tracks and measures the various interactions a customer has throughout their licencing experience. This was rolled out to over 500 staff at approximately 95 testing locations, delivering over 1.2M tests a year.
